Burger Recipe

Description

The most succulent burger recipe that you will ever have the pleasure of tasting! Packed with protein and cheeses, this burger will have you begging for more!

  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Burger Buns
  • Tomatoe
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Pound and mold ground beef into balls
  2. Push down on the balls on the frying pan and let cook
  3. Cut up veggies
  4. Once done cooking, place patties onto burger buns
  5. Place cheese and veggies onto the patty
  6. Enjoy!
